Energy Arc is a browser-based set planning tool for DJs. Paste your track list, generate a stage-based board, and rearrange songs by dragging them between phases — while the app scores every neighbor transition and keeps a live arc chart updated in real time. Everything runs locally in your browser: no account required, no data leaves your machine.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Import your tracks">
    Paste a track list from Excel or CSV — or load the built-in demo set to explore with sample data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate the board">
    Click **Generate Board** to auto-sort tracks into five phases (Introduction → Early Groove → Lift → Peak → Landing) following an ideal energy arc.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drag and refine">
    Drag cards up and down to reorder within a phase, or across columns to move a track to a different phase. Transition badges and the arc chart update instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and export">
    Save your board as JSON for later editing, or export a CSV when the sequence is ready for your crate.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Energy Arc is fully local. Your board is saved to your browser's localStorage on the machine you use. No server, no cloud sync, no account needed.
</Note>
